Welcome

The name Sudoux is a combination of two words in two different languages: the Italian part of the name was inspired by a friend of which always dressed modestly giving her a sense of softness to her style, while the French was on a trip to France back in 2019, it’s one of the few words learned despite the langue barrier as the French speakers described my sister’s hair. Sudoux is not like any other clothing brand. The story of Sudoux is more than a customized garment. See the idea was to sell Jalabiyas, aka “Thobes” in the Arab world, with a hint of modern western wear emerged to it. As a Sudanese born and raised Saudia Arabia, at the young age of 15 it was difficult for to relate to the country of citizenship despite not visiting the homeland once. First time wearing a Jalabiya it felt like pure hesitation, I thought of it more of a basic plain white garment worn by most African and Arab nations however I was naïve. Yes, in order to fit it must be worn; however, I disliked it. Everything about it. To me it was basic, but I understood the meaning behind the ‘Jalabiyah
